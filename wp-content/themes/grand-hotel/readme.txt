== Description ==

It’s always advised and is a better idea to choose a theme that has been designed for your business niche. If you’re a hotel owner, restaurateur, or are planning to start a hotel, snack bar, or café, etc. you ought to check out Grand Hotel WordPress Theme. It’s a template our design team has curated exclusively for this industry. There are several features that will give your business an edge when you present it using this ready-to-use template.
When you choose Grand Hotel WordPress Theme you get a template that offers a structured framework that helps you to streamline the presentation of your venture keeping in mind the convenience and navigation habit of your potential client visiting your website. We’ve designed Grand Hotel WordPress Theme in a way that it ensures key aspects related to your hotel or restaurant is covered. Such a layout ultimately increases your chances of success and attracts niche traffic your way. 
Besides being a visually great looking template it offers multiple functionalities that make the theme a pleasure for your client to visit, and explore your hotel online. At the same time you can manage the look and vibe of the site as you like without any difficulty.  
Let’s share all the features one by one. It’s a light weight theme, quick to load. We’ve used awesome images, relevant for your business to give it the vibe these are light weight. Visually Grand Hotel WordPress Theme is sophisticated, trendy, and modern. We’ve curated a responsive design. This ensures your visitor has a good experience exploring your hotel online, whether s/he uses mobile phone, laptop or any other device to visit your site.  
Behind the sophisticated look and advanced features that give it an upmarket feel is an easy interface or Dashboard which makes it easy even for a layman to adjust the feel and look by changing images and updating text from time to time without any technical or coding knowledge or experience. Within minutes you can adapt to the dashboard and understand how you can manage the template yourself. 
Using this dashboard saves you time, money and dependency on third party for making changes. When you download & install this WordPress Theme you know that your business will always give a freshly updated, crisp, and good energy vibe and offer a wonderful customer experience.
